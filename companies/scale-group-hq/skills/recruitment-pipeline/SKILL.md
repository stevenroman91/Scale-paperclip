# Recruitment Pipeline

End-to-end recruitment pipeline management for Scale Group, from sourcing through onboarding. This is a process and knowledge skill — it defines standardized workflows, evaluation frameworks, and compliance requirements.

## Pipeline Stages

```
Sourcing → Screening → Phone Screen → Technical Interview → Culture Fit → Offer → Onboarding
```

### Stage Definitions and SLAs

| Stage | Owner | SLA (max days) | Key Actions |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Sourcing** | Talent Acquisition | Ongoing | Identify and attract candidates |
| **Screening** | Talent Acquisition | 3 days | CV review, initial qualification |
| **Phone Screen** | Talent Acquisition | 5 days | 30-min call, motivation & fit check |
| **Technical Interview** | Hiring Manager | 7 days | Role-specific assessment (60-90 min) |
| **Culture Fit** | Team Lead + CHRO | 5 days | Values alignment, team dynamics (45-60 min) |
| **Offer** | CHRO + CFO | 3 days | Package preparation and negotiation |
| **Onboarding** | HR + Manager | 30 days | Integration program execution |

**Total target time-to-hire:** 25-35 days from sourcing to offer acceptance.

## Stage 1: Sourcing

### Channels

| Channel | Best For | Cost | Expected Volume |
|---|---|---|---|
| **LinkedIn Recruiter** | Senior roles, tech, sales | ~8,000 EUR/year license | 40-60% of pipeline |
| **Welcome to the Jungle** | Junior-mid roles, French market | ~3,000-5,000 EUR/year | 15-25% of pipeline |
| **Indeed France** | High-volume roles (SDR, support) | Pay-per-click or sponsored | 10-15% of pipeline |
| **Employee Referrals** | All roles (highest quality) | 1,000-2,000 EUR bonus on successful hire | 15-25% of pipeline |
| **Head Hunting** | C-level, VP, rare profiles | 20-30% of annual salary | 5-10% of pipeline |
| **Ecole partnerships** | Interns, alternance, juniors | Minimal | As needed |

### Sourcing Workflow

1. Hiring manager submits a job opening request with:
   - Role title, team, reporting line
   - Key responsibilities (5-7 bullets)
   - Must-have vs. nice-to-have requirements
   - Salary range (validated by CFO)
   - Start date target
2. Talent Acquisition creates the job posting:
   - Write job description following Scale Group template (inclusive language, clear structure)
   - Publish on selected channels based on role type
   - Set up LinkedIn Recruiter search with Boolean queries
3. Track all sourced candidates in the pipeline with:
   - Source channel (for ROI analysis)
   - Date first contacted
   - Initial response (interested / not interested / no response)

## Stage 2: Screening

### CV Review Criteria

Evaluate each application on a pass/fail basis:

| Criteria | Pass | Fail |
|---|---|---|
| Relevant experience | Matches 70%+ of must-haves | Matches < 50% of must-haves |
| Education | Meets minimum requirement or equivalent experience | No relevant background |
| Location | Within commute range or open to remote (if applicable) | Cannot meet location requirement |
| Language | French fluent + English professional (minimum for Scale Group) | Insufficient language level |
| Career trajectory | Logical progression, stable tenure (avg > 18 months) | Multiple short stints without explanation |

### Screening Scorecard (per role type)

**SDR / Business Development:**

| Competency | Weight | Evaluation Criteria |
|---|---|---|
| Communication skills | 25% | Written application quality, clarity |
| Sales aptitude | 25% | Prior prospecting or customer-facing experience |
| Resilience indicators | 20% | Sports, competitive background, progression despite challenges |
| Tech savviness | 15% | CRM usage, SaaS tool familiarity |
| Cultural fit signals | 15% | Values alignment visible in CV/cover letter |

**Account Manager / Account Executive:**

| Competency | Weight | Evaluation Criteria |
|---|---|---|
| Sales track record | 30% | Quota attainment, deal sizes, revenue generated |
| Industry knowledge | 20% | B2B experience, relevant verticals |
| Relationship management | 20% | Client retention, account growth evidence |
| Strategic thinking | 15% | Complex deal navigation, stakeholder mapping |
| Team collaboration | 15% | Cross-functional project experience |

**Tech (Developer / Engineer):**

| Competency | Weight | Evaluation Criteria |
|---|---|---|
| Technical skills | 35% | Languages, frameworks, relevant stack experience |
| Problem-solving | 25% | Open source contributions, technical projects, hackathons |
| Architecture awareness | 20% | System design experience, scalability considerations |
| Team fit | 10% | Agile experience, code review culture |
| Communication | 10% | Technical writing, documentation habits |

## Stage 3: Phone Screen

**Duration:** 30 minutes.
**Conducted by:** Talent Acquisition.

### Standard Phone Screen Script

1. **Introduction** (2 min): Present Scale Group, the role, the interview process.
2. **Candidate background** (10 min):
   - Walk me through your career path.
   - What motivated your application / interest in this role?
3. **Role fit** (10 min):
   - What experience do you have with [key requirement]?
   - Describe a recent relevant accomplishment.
4. **Logistics** (5 min):
   - Availability / notice period
   - Salary expectations (provide range if candidate asks first)
   - Location / remote work preferences
5. **Questions from candidate** (3 min)

### Phone Screen Evaluation

Score 1-5 on each dimension:

| Dimension | 1 (Reject) | 3 (Borderline) | 5 (Strong) |
|---|---|---|---|
| Motivation | Unclear / generic | Reasonable interest | Deep research, specific enthusiasm |
| Communication | Hard to follow, unfocused | Adequate, some structure | Clear, concise, engaging |
| Relevant experience | No relevant experience | Some transferable skills | Direct, strong match |
| Salary alignment | >20% above budget | Within 10% of range | Within range |

**Pass threshold:** Average score >= 3.5 with no dimension below 2.

## Stage 4: Technical Interview

**Duration:** 60-90 minutes.
**Conducted by:** Hiring Manager + one senior team member.

### Format by Role Type

**Sales roles (SDR, AE, AM):**
- Role play: cold call simulation (15 min)
- Case study: analyze a prospect scenario and build an outreach strategy (30 min)
- Metrics discussion: how they track pipeline, forecast, prioritize (15 min)

**Tech roles:**
- Live coding or take-home assignment review (30-45 min)
- System design discussion (20 min)
- Technical Q&A on past projects (15 min)

**Operations / Finance roles:**
- Case study: solve a real business problem (30 min)
- Tool proficiency demonstration (15 min)
- Process design exercise (15 min)

### Technical Scorecard

Score 1-5 on each competency:

| Competency | Weight | Description |
|---|---|---|
| Core technical skills | 30% | Direct skills for the role |
| Problem-solving approach | 25% | Methodology, structured thinking |
| Depth of knowledge | 20% | Beyond surface-level, edge cases |
| Learning agility | 15% | How they handle unknown territory |
| Collaboration style | 10% | How they work through problems with others |

**Pass threshold:** Weighted average >= 3.5 with core technical skills >= 3.

## Stage 5: Culture Fit

**Duration:** 45-60 minutes.
**Conducted by:** Team Lead + CHRO (or HR representative).

### Scale Group Values Assessment

| Value | Interview Question | Strong Answer Signals |
|---|---|---|
| **Ownership** | Tell me about a time you took responsibility for something beyond your job description. | Proactive initiative, accountability, follow-through |
| **Transparency** | How do you handle sharing bad news with your team or manager? | Direct communication, constructive framing |
| **Growth mindset** | What's something you failed at recently and what did you learn? | Honest self-reflection, concrete learning |
| **Collaboration** | Describe a project where you had to work closely with a team you didn't choose. | Adaptability, conflict resolution, positive outcome |
| **Client obsession** | How do you balance client demands with internal priorities? | Empathy, prioritization, creative solutions |

**Pass threshold:** No value scored below 3. At least 3 values scored 4+.

## Stage 6: Offer Management

### Salary Benchmarking

Use the following data sources:
- Figures.hr (French tech salary benchmarks)
- Glassdoor France
- LinkedIn Salary Insights
- Internal salary grid (Scale Group compensation bands)

### Offer Package Components

| Component | Details |
|---|---|
| **Base salary** | Monthly gross, Syntec classification (position + coefficient) |
| **Variable pay** | Sales: 70/30 or 80/20 split (base/variable). Others: 0-15% annual bonus |
| **Equity / BSPCE** | For senior roles: BSPCE plan (if applicable), vesting over 4 years with 1-year cliff |
| **Benefits** | Mutuelle Alan (family option), titres restaurant (9.50 EUR/day, 60% employer), RTT (8-12 days/year under Syntec), transport (50% Navigo or forfait mobilite) |
| **Remote work** | Policy: 2-3 days remote per week (role-dependent) |

### Negotiation Playbook

1. **Initial offer:** Position at 50th-65th percentile of market range.
2. **If candidate counters:** Maximum flexibility up to 75th percentile; above requires CFO approval.
3. **Non-salary levers:** Additional remote days, training budget, start date flexibility, job title adjustment, sign-on bonus for exceptional candidates.
4. **Deadline:** Give candidate 5 business days to respond. Follow up at day 3.
5. **Rejection handling:** Ask for feedback, keep relationship warm for future openings.

## Stage 7: Onboarding

### Pre-arrival Checklist (HR)

| Task | Owner | Deadline |
|---|---|---|
| Send employment contract (CDI/CDD) | HR | 5 days before start |
| Collect signed contract + documents (RIB, carte vitale, diploma) | HR | 2 days before start |
| DPAE declaration (Declaration Prealable a l'Embauche) | HR | Before start date |
| Order laptop + equipment | IT / Office Manager | 3 days before start |
| Create email + tool accounts (Slack, Notion, CRM, etc.) | IT | 1 day before start |
| Assign buddy (same team, different role) | Manager | Before start |
| Schedule Week 1 meetings (manager, team, cross-functional) | HR + Manager | Before start |

### Week 1 Program

| Day | Activity |
|---|---|
| **Day 1** | Welcome breakfast, HR admin (mutuelle, transport), company overview presentation, IT setup, tool walkthroughs |
| **Day 2** | Team introductions, product/service deep dive, access to ScaleAcademy training modules |
| **Day 3** | Role-specific training begins, first 1:1 with manager (set 30-60-90 day goals) |
| **Day 4** | Shadow a senior team member, start assigned ScaleAcademy track |
| **Day 5** | Week 1 recap with buddy, feedback to HR |

### 30-60-90 Day Framework

| Period | Focus | Success Criteria |
|---|---|---|
| **30 days** | Learn | Understand company, product, team, tools. Complete onboarding training. | 
| **60 days** | Contribute | Start delivering on role responsibilities with guidance. Hit first small milestones. |
| **90 days** | Perform | Autonomous on core tasks. End of probation validation. |

### Periode d'Essai (Probation Period)

Per Syntec convention collective:

| Status | Initial Period | Renewal | Total Maximum |
|---|---|---|---|
| Non-cadre (ETAM) | 2 months | 1 month | 3 months |
| Cadre | 4 months | 4 months | 8 months |

Renewal must be notified in writing before the end of the initial period. Termination during probation requires notice: 24h (< 8 days), 48h (8 days-1 month), 2 weeks (1-3 months), 1 month (> 3 months).

### Conversion Rate Benchmarks

| Stage Transition | Target Rate | Alert if Below |
|---|---|---|
| Sourcing → Screening | 30-40% | 20% |
| Screening → Phone Screen | 40-50% | 30% |
| Phone Screen → Technical | 50-60% | 40% |
| Technical → Culture Fit | 60-70% | 50% |
| Culture Fit → Offer | 70-80% | 60% |
| Offer → Acceptance | 80-90% | 70% |
| **Overall Sourcing → Hire** | **5-10%** | **3%** |

### Tracking Requirements

For each candidate, record:
- Current stage and date entered
- Time spent in each completed stage
- Rejection reason (if rejected) — categorized: skills gap, culture mismatch, salary mismatch, candidate withdrew, better offer elsewhere
- Source channel
- Interviewer scores at each stage

## Legal Compliance (French Labor Law)

- **Non-discrimination:** Article L1132-1 Code du travail. Never filter by age, gender, origin, disability, religion, sexual orientation, family status, political opinions, or union membership.
- **RGPD / data retention:** Candidate data must be deleted 2 years after last contact if no hiring (CNIL recommendation). Inform candidates of data processing.
- **Response obligation:** Respond to every application (Article L1222-4). Minimum: confirmation of receipt + final outcome notification.
- **CDI vs CDD rules:** CDD only for specific legal reasons (replacement, seasonal, temporary activity increase). Maximum 18 months including renewals. Indemnite de precarite: 10% of total gross.
- **Convention collective Syntec:** Respect minimum salaries per coefficient, probation periods, notice periods.
- **Promesse d'embauche:** A formal written offer with position, salary, and start date constitutes a binding promise under French law. Cannot be withdrawn without consequences.
